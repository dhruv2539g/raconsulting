About Rahul Agarwal

Rahul Agarwal is a Distribution Expert, Growth Leader and Change Catalyst with over three decades of leadership experience across banking, insurance, operations, risk management and business transformation.

Most recently President – Institutional Business at Bajaj Allianz Life Insurance and previously Chief Distribution Officer at Exide Life Insurance.

His leadership experience spans Bajaj Allianz Life Insurance, Exide Life Insurance, ING Life Insurance, HDFC Bank, Bank of America and Modi Xerox.

A rare blend of frontline sales leadership, operational excellence and strategic transformation experience enables Rahul to help organisations unlock growth and build sustainable competitive advantage.
